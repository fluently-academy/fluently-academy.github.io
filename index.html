Common Questions

How do I pay for my lessons?

Once you book a slot, you will receive payment instructions. We currently accept PayPal and bank transfers. Lessons must be paid 24h in advance.

What is the cancellation policy?

Lessons cancelled with less than 24 hours' notice are non-refundable. If the teacher cancels, you get a full refund.
