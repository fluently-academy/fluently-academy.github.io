Community Rules & Code of Conduct

To ensure a safe, supportive, and productive learning environment in our Google Chat community, all members must follow these rules:

1. Be Respectful & Supportive Always be kind to your fellow students and teachers. We believe in a positive atmosphere where everyone feels safe to speak and make mistakes.
2. English-Only Practice To maximize your fluency and confidence, all conversations in the community must be in English. Don't worry about perfect grammar—just communicate!
3. No Spam or Advertising Sharing external links, promoting other services, or sending repetitive unsolicited messages is strictly prohibited.
4. Privacy & Safety Respect the privacy of other members. Do not share personal information of others without their explicit consent.
5. Constructive Positivity Focus on helping each other grow. Our system is built on emotional control and positivity to help everyone build confidence in their skills.
